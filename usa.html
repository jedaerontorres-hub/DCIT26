United States

The USA has some of the most advanced fighter jets.


  1. F-22 Raptor (Air Superiority Fighter)

    2. The F-22 Raptor is designed to control the skies and defeat enemy aircraft. It uses stealth technology, making it hard to detect by radar.
    It is very fast, highly maneuverable, and considered one of the most advanced fighter jets in the world.

  2. F-35 Lightning II (Multirole Fighter)

    3. The F-35 Lightning II is a modern jet that can perform many different missions. It can fight enemy aircraft, attack ground targets, and gather intelligence.
    It also has stealth capabilities and advanced sensors for better battlefield awareness.

  3. F-16 Fighting Falcon (Multirole Fighter)

    4. The F-16 Fighting Falcon is a lightweight and versatile jet. It is used for both air-to-air combat and ground attack missions.
    It is one of the most widely used fighter jets globally because of its reliability and effectiveness.
