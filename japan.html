Japan

Japan's fighter jets focus on advanced technology, speed, and strong air defense to protect its national airspace.


  1. F-15J Eagle (Air Superiority Fighter)

    2. The F-15J is Japan's main air defense fighter. Its role is to protect the country's airspace by intercepting and destroying enemy aircraft.
    It is fast, powerful, and highly reliable.

  2. F-2 (Multirole Fighter)

    3. the F-2 is a fighter jet developed by Japan based on the F-16. It is used for both air combat and ground attack missions.
    It also has advanced radar and can carry anti-ship missiles.

  3. F-35A Lightning II (Stealth Multirole Fighter)

    4. Japan uses the F-35A for modern air defense.
    It is a stealth aircraft that can perform multiple missions, including air combat, ground attack, and reconnaissance.
