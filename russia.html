Russia

Russia is known for powerful and fast jets.


  1. Su-57 (Stealth Multirole Fighter)

    2. The Su-57 is Russia's most advanced stealth fighter jet. It is designed to perform multiple missions such as air combat and ground attack.
    It combines speed, stealth, and advanced weapons to compete with modern Western aircraft.

  2. Su-35 (Air Superiority Fighter)

    3. The Su-35 is a highly maneuverable jet built for air-to-air combat.
    It has powerful engines and advanced radar systems, allowing it to detect and engage enemy aircraft from long distances.

  3. MiG-29 (Multirole Fighter)

    4. The MiG-29 is a fast and agile fighter jet used for both air combat and ground attack missions.
    It has been widely used by many countries and is known for its strong performance in dogfights.
